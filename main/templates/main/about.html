What we do

Sequence Control Panel (SCP) is a dynamic dashboard created to help people new to investing.
Our dashboard is designed to make it easy for people to get into the market and grow their portfolio.
We developed unique algorithms that monitor stock and forecast results to our investors.
Our data is powered by Wall Street giving us an edge when forecasting and predicting stock prices.

SCp background

Investing in the stock market is a great way to save up for retirement or buy a new house.
So whats stopping people from doing it?
Looking at investor applications available across the web, they aren't very user friendly.
If you're not a stock broker or work in the financial field, it can be overwhelming.
That's why we developed SCP. Our dynamic dashboard is built so everyday people can use our visually powered analytic service to help them invest within the market.
