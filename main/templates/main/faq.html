General Questions

What is Sequence Control Panel?

HouseMyDog is an online community where Dog Parents can connect with Dog Sittes, to find safe and loving real homes for their dogs while they travel.

Here at HouseMyDog we love our dogs like members of the family. We weren't happy with the traditional boarding options, which were overly complicated, expensive and left us feeling guilty leaving our pups behind when we travelled. We wanted to fix that. We dreamed of a simple, cage-free and stress free solution for safely boarding our dogs in real homes at affordable rates, and from that the idea HouseMyDog was born.

With HouseMyDog you can chose from hundreds of sitters, ranging from professional pet sitters to dog lovers who will mind your dog in their own home, provide one on one attention and love your precious pup like it was their own, leaving you to travel with peace of mind. You can arrange to meet your host in advance of your pups stay, and stay in touch with photo updates, so you can rest at ease that they are enjoying their holiday as much as you are!

How does Sequence Control Panel work?

If you are leaving home and you can't bring your pet with you, but want someone responsible and loving to care for your dog, then you've come to the right place. It's easy to use HouseMyDog; all you have to do is create a free HouseMyDog account, enter the location and dates when you want your dog minded, and then search through hundreds of screened and experienced Sitters. With just one click you can email your perfect Sitter, schedule a Meet and Greet, and make a reservation at any time.

HouseMyDog will only charge your card once a Sitter accepts your request, and after you book we'll put you in touch with the Sitter directly to arrange the finer details. Click here (link) to watch our 90 second "How It Works" video and learn more about HouseMyDog.

Where do you get your data from?

The amount of information we need depends on what you want to do on our site. If you want to board your dog we ask that you share basic information about your dog (such as health and behavioural info) so that our sitters have all the information they need to provide exceptional care. If you want to mind other dogs, we'll need information about your experience with animals and your home. We will never share your last name, email address, physical address, or phone number until after a booking is accepted. For more information please check our sitter FAQ below.

Do I have to pay to join Sequence Control Panel?

No way! Sequence Control Panel is free to join and ALWAYS will be. It costs nothing to become a member, create a profile and engage with data.

What is the Sequence Control Panel guarantee?

Here at Sequence Control Panel, we are committed to providing the best financial analytics solutions. The Sequence Control Panel guarantee will offer you peace of mind while investing. We do not guarantee the forecasts presented but we stand by our data. SCP guarantees 24/7 support to all users.

Dog Owners

Why is HouseMyDog right for my dog?

We believe that all dogs should be taken care of by loving sitters, in real homes with lots of individual attention. If you hate the idea of your dog staying in an overcrowded kennel while you're away then HouseMyDog is the perfect solution for you. Our service enables you to browse through hundreds of sitters to find the perfect home for your pooch while you travel.

Is HouseMyDog safe?

Absolutely! HouseMyDog is committed to providing a secure and safe environment for all the Dog Parents and Sitters in our community. We are very selective with our Sitter community; every sitter has been screened and approved by our team. Our sitters range from professional pet-sitters to dog owners, who can all provide a loving home from home for your precious pup. We require all Sitters to provide a detailed profile describing their services and experience, including pictures and customer reviews.

All stays booked and paid for on HouseMyDog are covered by our HouseMyDog Terms of Service, including HouseMyDog's Protection Guarantee. We also encourage you to have a meet and greet prior to your stay to introduce your pup to their new friends and make sure they're compatible.

How do I pick my perfect sitter?

All sitters on HouseMyDog are approved and screened by our team. In addition, there are also multiple verification credentials that sitters can earn, look for these to find our most qualified sitters:

Phone Interview complete - The sitter has passed a rigorous phone interview performed by the experts at our Concierge.

Paypal setup - The sitter has provided more verification by connecting his paypal account to his HouseMyDog account.

Facebook connected - The sitter has verified their identity by connecting their Facebook profile with their HouseMyDog profile.

3 References provided - The sitter has provided 3 valid references outlining their experience caring for dogs.

Police Clearance Cert – The sitter has provided HouseMyDohg with an official police vetting certificate.

ID Screened – The sitter has provided an official ID to verify their identity with HouseMyDog

Parent reviews – The sitter has received positive feedback from other dog parents in the HouseMyDog community - only real parents can write reviews

What is the cancellation policy?

Each of our hosts set their own cancellation policy, which is clearly stated on their profiles. There are two options:

Flexible: Full refund (excluding fees) 3 days prior to arrival, 50% refund (excluding fees) after that.

Strict: Full refund (excluding fees) 7 days prior to arrival, 50% refund (excluding fees) after that.

Please make note of a sitter's cancellation policy when booking, to avoid any issues in the event a cancellation must be made.

Do I need to be licensed and insured?

You do not need to be licensed or insured to use HouseMyDog.com. If your dog encounters any problems, you have access to the HouseMyDog guarantee which provides both 24/7 support and Complimentary Emergency Vet Care.

What if I want to meet the sitter before I leave my dog?

No problem! We encourage parents to schedule a Meet and Greet appointment with their prospective sitter prior to the stay to sort out the finer details and make sure their pup is compatible with any resident dogs as well as the sitter themselves.

What if my dog has special requirements?

We've made it easy for Dog Parents and Sitters to get in touch and learn more about each other through HouseMyDog.com. All of our sitters' profiles are full of helpful information and there are plenty of sitters who can cater to your pup's individual needs. You are always encouraged to message sitters or schedule a Meet & Greet with your sitter to introduce your pets and sort out any special needs in advance of the stay.

What should I bring when I drop off my dog?

In order for your dog to feel at home in their sitters house, we recommend keeping their habits and routine as similar to home as possible. Don't forget to bring enough food for the entire duration of the stay unless the sitter specifies otherwise, as well as their leash and any medicine they may require. We do not recommend bringing scented items or toys with huge sentimental value if there are other dogs present during the stay, as this may result in territorial behaviour and possible damage to the toy/item.

How do I cancel my reservation?

We understand that sometimes unforeseen circumstances will force you to change or cancel an existing reservation. We want to make that process easy for you. To alter or cancel an existing reservation, please cancel with your sitter first, then call our support at IRE 01 905 9056 / UK 020 3393 5921 or email us at support@housemydog.com. Please remember to include your receipt number in the subject line.

How does the payment work?

HouseMyDog has made paying for a great stay simple. We accept all major credit cards – Visa, MasterCard, and American Express. All you have to do is select the perfect sitter for your pup, click the "Make Reservation" button on the sitter's profile page and follow the simple steps to enter credit card information. Your card will be charged when a reservation is confirmed. Your money will then be held safely until 48h after the end of the stay, in order to insure the sitter delivered the service adequately.

How much is the service fee?

Dog owners do not pay any additional charges other than the dog sitters cost per service.

Dog Sitters

Do I need to provide food or items for the dog?

Not necessarily. Unless you specify otherwise, it is the owners responsibility to provide food, bedding, or any other items/medicines their pet may require on their stay. However if you wish to provide any of these items you may do so and prearrange it with the owner.

Can I control which dogs I accept for sitting and boarding?

Absolutely! You have full control of what dogs you want to host. All these preferences can be chosen in your profile based on your own requirements. Don't worry, we won't take it personally if Charlie the Chihuahua doesn't want to share his home with Gary the Great Dane!

What if I want to offer other services?

We are currently working on a function to enable sitters to offer add-on services such as grooming, walking, training etc. If you wish to offer extra services, we recommend you specify your abilities/services on your profile and adjust your price per night accordingly.

How and when can I get paid?

Our payment process is operated securely by PayPal. HouseMyDog will receive parent's payment upon confirmation of the stay, and will transfer the money to you 48 hours after the end of the stay. It is imperative you do not take direct payment from parents as this renders the HouseMyDog Guarantee void. To create your own free PayPal account click here.

How much is the service fee?

Signing up to become a "Sitter" is simple and free. You set your own rates, control your schedule, and decide which dogs to accept.

There is a 15% service fee which enables HouseMyDog to bring customers to your profile, provide secure online payments, and offer free 24/7 Support and Emergency Vet Care with every booking. Without this fee we would not be able to provide and maintain the HouseMyDog community.

What if I want to cancel a reservation?

If you need to cancel a reservation, please contact our support team at support@housemydog.com or call us on IRE 01 905 9056 / UK 020 3393 5921.

What should I do in case of emergency?

In the event of an emergency contact our 24/7 support team at IRE 01 905 9056 / UK 020 3393 5921.
